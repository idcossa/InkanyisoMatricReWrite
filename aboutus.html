what makes us special?


We re-create a fresh foundation for our students which provide an in-depths understanding of all subjects for a higher points in all examinations (NSC/SCE).

We value those who wish to build a better community while increasing their skills and experience in the field of teaching.

"We all have dreams. But in order to make our dreams come into reality, it takes an awful lot of determination, dedication, self-discipline and effort."

The transformative learning process at Inkanyiso Matric ReWrite is designed to help our students grow both in and out of the classroom. Each day is filled with an opportunities to experience new educational and social situations for further development. Our teachers create a safe and open setting, where they can guide students in exploring the world around them.
