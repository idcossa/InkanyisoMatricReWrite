FREQUENLY ASKED QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS
What Is Inkanyiso Matric Rewrite?
  • Inkanyiso is a finishing school that teach and prepare students who previously matriculated in any high secondary school, those who wish to upgrade their results. We offer classes in time stipulated by our management.
  • Inkanyiso Matric Rewrite refers to a program in South Africa designed to assist students who did not pass their Matric (Grade 12) exams to rewrite them and obtain their Matric certificate. The program is offered by Inkanyiso Education and Development, a private education institution that provides supplementary education services.
  • The Inkanyiso Matric Rewrite program also provides study materials, practice exams, and support to help students prepare for their exams. The program is flexible, and students can choose to attend classes in person or online, depending on their preferences and circumstances.

Is Studying at Inkanyiso Matric ReWrite Full Time or Part Time?
  • Students are expected to attend on the full-time basis during afternoon classes.

Who qualifiers for enrollment?
  • We have two categories of qualifying students. Student must have written matric prior.
  • Category 1: Students who wish to upgrade their matric results.
  • Category 2: Students who failed their matric.

How long is the study period at IMR?
  • We coordinate classes from January to November for each academic year.

Do IMR Offers / coordinate Final Examination?
  • We do not coordinate the final examination, students are expected to register at their prospective schools that are legally permitted by Department of Education to coordinate such.

Obtaining Matric Certificate.
  • Inkanyiso does not by any chance offer Grade 12 Certificate.

How the program is designed.
  • The program is open to students who did not pass their Matric exams or did not meet the minimum requirements to obtain their certificate. It offers classes in various subjects such as Mathematics, English, Physical Science, and Life Sciences. The classes are taught by experienced teachers who provide personalized attention to each student to ensure they understand the concepts and improve their knowledge and skills.

What i need to know about imr.
  • Inkanyiso Matric Rewrite program is a valuable initiative that provides a lifeline to students who need a second chance to obtain their Matric certificate and improve their future prospects.
